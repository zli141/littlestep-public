Questions, answered

How is my family's data kept private?

Everything you create is stored in Apple iCloud (CloudKit) under your own account, and shared only with the family members you explicitly invite to a baby's circle. There are no ad-driven feeds and no outside companies with access to your content.

How do I invite family members?

In the app, go to Settings → Family → "Invite Family" and share the secure iCloud link (Messages, AirDrop, etc.). Family members open it on their iPhone while signed in to iCloud. As the owner, you can set each person to view-only or edit, or remove them anytime.

Is LittleStep free?

Yes. LittleStep is free to use. An optional auto-renewing membership removes ads for non-subscribers; you can manage or cancel anytime in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Can I track more than one baby?

Absolutely. You can add multiple babies, switch between them, and even use a different nickname for yourself in each family circle.

What happens if I delete a baby or the app?

Deleting a baby in the app permanently removes its photos, records, and family sharing. Uninstalling the app clears locally cached data; your iCloud data follows Apple's iCloud controls.
